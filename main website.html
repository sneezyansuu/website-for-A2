Innovation & Creativity Against Diesel Mining

A comprehensive marketing campaign showcasing creative solutions to combat the environmental impact of diesel fuel in the mining industry.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to fighting against the harmful use of diesel fuels in the mining industry through innovative marketing and creative solutions. Our campaign aims to raise awareness about the environmental impact of diesel mining and promote sustainable alternatives.

Through our five key projects - logo design, poster campaign, informative podcast, branded household items, and statement clothing - we're building a movement that demands change in the mining sector.

Our Projects

Five innovative approaches to spread awareness and drive change in the mining industry.

Logo Design

Our distinctive logo features a red circle with a yellow star, symbolizing urgency and hope. The red background represents the critical nature of our mission, while the star signifies guidance toward a better future.

Brand Identity

Poster Campaign

A series of high-impact posters featuring stark imagery of mining environmental damage contrasted with sustainable alternatives. Bold typography and our signature red and yellow color scheme create immediate visual impact.

Visual Communication

3-Minute Podcast

Our concise, informative podcast series delivers powerful messages about diesel mining impacts and alternatives. Each episode features industry experts and affected community members sharing their perspectives.

Audio Content

Household Items

Everyday products reimagined with our campaign branding. From coffee mugs to reusable shopping bags, these items spread awareness through daily use while demonstrating sustainable material alternatives.

Product Design

Clothing Line

A collection of statement apparel featuring our logo and powerful messaging. Each piece is made from sustainable materials, creating a walking billboard for our cause while demonstrating ethical production.

Wearable Activism
